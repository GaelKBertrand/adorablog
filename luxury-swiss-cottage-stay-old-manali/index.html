Where the Beas Meets Luxury: The Adora Swiss Cottage Experience

Traditional camping in the Himalayas usually involves a tradeoff: you get the views, but you lose the comfort. The Adora Swiss Cottage was designed to end that compromise. Located on the quieter, upper reaches of Manali, these are permanent luxury sanctuaries that allow you to wake up in the wild without leaving your king-sized bed.

Not Your Average Tent: The Technical Specs

We don't do "basic" camping. Each Swiss Cottage is a weather-proof structure designed to handle the temperamental Manali climate while maintaining an upscale interior.

  • High-Vaulted Ceilings: To prevent that cramped "tent feel" and allow for better airflow.
  • Premium Insulation: Wooden flooring keeps the ground chill away, paired with high-grade canvas walls.
  • The "Real" Bathroom: Every cottage features a private, attached bathroom with high-pressure hot water—a non-negotiable for anyone who has actually trekked in Himachal.

The Manali Advantage

You are five minutes away from the bohemian soul of Manali’s cafes, yet shielded by a private grove of cedar trees. This makes the Swiss Cottage the premier choice for "Workations." With high-speed Wi-Fi and a private veranda that acts as your office, you can attend a Zoom call with the Pir Panjal range as your background, not a filter.
