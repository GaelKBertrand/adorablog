The Family Sanctuary in Himalayas: Starway Homes

Your Home in the Clouds: Why Starway Homes Beats a Hotel

When you’re traveling with a family or a group of six friends, three separate hotel rooms feel disconnected. Starway Homes by Adora Hospitality provides a sophisticated alternative: premium, fully-furnished mountain flats that offer the privacy of a residence with the service of a luxury hotel.

Designed for Connection and Productivity

We’ve built Starway Homes for the modern group traveler. We understand that "space" is the ultimate luxury in the mountains.

  • Expansive Living Areas: Large communal spaces for group bonding, far exceeding the square footage of any luxury suite in the valley.
  • Equipped Kitchenettes: The flexibility to brew your own tea or prepare a midnight snack provides a level of comfort that "room service" can't match.
  • Long-Stay Optimized: With dedicated parking, ergonomic workstations, and high-speed fiber connectivity, this is the gold standard for long-term Himalayan rentals.

The Adora Ecosystem

What sets Starway apart is the integration. Guests at Starway Homes have direct, priority access to Adora Treks for organizing day-hikes to Bhrigu Lake or Hampta Pass. You aren't just renting a flat; you’re tapping into a network of local Himalayan experts.
